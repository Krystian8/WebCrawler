Lalit Mohan plays to be staged today

Tribune News Service

Dehradun, December 26

As part of Rangkarm Sadhana – 2016, two plays — Muthbhed and Vidrohi — of playwright, director and author late Lalit Mohan Thapalyal will be staged in the Dehradun Municipal Auditorium on December 27.

A cultural organisation titled Sadhana Sanskriti Pratishthan has been conducting this Rangkarm Sadhana event in the memory of its founder Lalit Mohan Thapalyal since 2005. A native of Khatsyun Shrikot village in Pauri Garhwal, Lalit Mohan Thapalyal was the recipient of several awards, including the Garhwali Bhasha Parishad, Jaishree Samman, Children’s Book Trust award for drama and the Mohan Rakesh Samman of the Sahitya Kala Parishad, Delhi. His plays in Garhwali and Hindi are well known. They highlight not only his love for his native region, but also his concerns towards the state of society and the human condition.

Before Dehradun, the two plays have already been staged in Pauri and Delhi.

Sadhana Sanskriti Pratishthan’s trustee Anjana Rajan said the organisation had been working for promotion of theatre across the country. She said special theatre workshops for children and adults were conducted at the organisation’s Sahibabad studio. She also disclosed that it was for the first time that Rangkarm Sadhana was staged in Pauri recently.
